AAROGYASRI HEALTH SCHEME (Telangana and Andhra Pradesh)
Eligibility: White ration card holders, BPL families
Coverage: Up to Rs 5 lakh per family per year for 1059+ medical procedures
Covered conditions: Cancer, heart disease, kidney disease, neurosurgery, burns, accidents, organ transplants
How to apply: Visit nearest empanelled hospital with white ration card and Aadhaar card
Documents needed: White ration card, Aadhaar card, income certificate

PM-JAY (Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana / Ayushman Bharat)
Eligibility: Families listed in SECC 2011 database as deprived, BPL families
Coverage: Up to Rs 5 lakh per family per year, cashless treatment
Covered conditions: Secondary and tertiary care hospitalization, surgeries, ICU
How to apply: Check eligibility on pmjay.gov.in or visit Ayushman Bharat kiosk at hospital
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, ration card, PM-JAY eligibility letter if available

NTR VAIDYA SEVA (Andhra Pradesh)
Eligibility: White ration card holders in Andhra Pradesh
Coverage: Cashless treatment up to Rs 5 lakh for listed procedures
Covered conditions: Similar to Aarogyasri - cardiac, cancer, neuro, renal procedures
How to apply: Visit empanelled network hospital with ration card

NHM (National Health Mission) - AP and Telangana
Eligibility: All citizens, special focus on rural and underserved areas
Services: Maternal health, child immunization, family planning, disease control programs
How to access: Visit nearest PHC (Primary Health Center) or government hospital
Services are free at government facilities under NHM

JANANI SURAKSHA YOJANA
Eligibility: Pregnant women, especially BPL families
Coverage: Cash assistance for institutional delivery
Benefit: Rs 700-1400 depending on rural/urban and institution type
How to apply: Register at Anganwadi center or PHC during pregnancy

YSR AAROGYASRI (Andhra Pradesh - updated scheme name)
Eligibility: All families with annual income below Rs 5 lakh
Coverage: Cashless treatment up to Rs 5 lakh per year
Covered conditions: 2400+ procedures including complex surgeries
How to apply: Aarogya Suraksha health cards distributed through camps, or apply at MeeSeva centers

DR YSR KANTI VELUGU (Andhra Pradesh)
Eligibility: All citizens, eye care focus
Coverage: Free eye checkups, free spectacles, free cataract surgery
How to apply: Visit health camps or government hospitals

KCR KIT / AMMA ODI (Telangana)
Eligibility: Pregnant and lactating women in Telangana
Coverage: Nutrition kit and financial assistance for institutional delivery
Benefit: Rs 12,000-13,000 plus a kit of essential items
How to apply: Register at government hospital during pregnancy

108 EMERGENCY AMBULANCE SERVICE
Eligibility: All citizens, free emergency transport
Coverage: Free ambulance service for medical emergencies
How to access: Call 108 for emergency ambulance

104 HEALTH HELPLINE
Eligibility: All citizens
Coverage: Free medical advice, mobile medical units in rural areas
How to access: Call 104 for health advice and mobile clinic schedule

ESI SCHEME (Employees State Insurance)
Eligibility: Organized sector workers earning below wage ceiling
Coverage: Medical care, cash benefits during sickness, maternity benefits
How to apply: Through employer registration

CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme)
Eligibility: Central government employees and pensioners
Coverage: Comprehensive medical care including OPD, hospitalization, medicines
How to apply: Through employer/pension department

DISABILITY PENSION SCHEME
Eligibility: Persons with 40% or more disability
Coverage: Monthly pension assistance
How to apply: Apply at Mandal Revenue Office with disability certificate

OLD AGE PENSION SCHEME
Eligibility: Citizens above 60 years (varies by state), BPL preference
Coverage: Monthly pension assistance
How to apply: Apply through local Mandal/Municipal office
