PARACETAMOL (Acetaminophen)
Used for: Fever, mild to moderate pain, headache, body ache
Dosage: 500mg-1000mg every 4-6 hours, maximum 4000mg per day
Take after food
Side effects: Rare at normal doses, liver damage if overdosed
Avoid: Alcohol while taking this medicine

METFORMIN
Used for: Type 2 diabetes management
Dosage: 500mg-1000mg twice daily with meals
Take after food
Side effects: Stomach upset, diarrhea, metallic taste
Avoid: Should not be stopped suddenly, monitor blood sugar regularly

AMLODIPINE
Used for: High blood pressure, chest pain (angina)
Dosage: 5mg-10mg once daily
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Swelling in ankles, dizziness, flushing
Avoid: Grapefruit juice may increase side effects

AMOXICILLIN
Used for: Bacterial infections - throat, ear, chest, urinary tract
Dosage: 500mg three times daily for 5-7 days as prescribed
Take with or without food
Side effects: Diarrhea, nausea, allergic rash
Avoid: Complete full course even if feeling better; do not skip doses

AZITHROMYCIN
Used for: Bacterial infections - respiratory, skin, ear infections
Dosage: 500mg once daily for 3 days, or as prescribed
Take on empty stomach, 1 hour before or 2 hours after food
Side effects: Stomach upset, diarrhea
Avoid: Antacids within 2 hours of taking this medicine

CETIRIZINE
Used for: Allergies, runny nose, itching, hives
Dosage: 10mg once daily, preferably at night
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Drowsiness, dry mouth
Avoid: Alcohol, driving if feeling drowsy

OMEPRAZOLE
Used for: Acid reflux, stomach ulcers, heartburn, GERD
Dosage: 20mg-40mg once daily before breakfast
Take on empty stomach, 30 minutes before food
Side effects: Headache, stomach pain, nausea
Avoid: Long-term use without doctor supervision

IBUPROFEN
Used for: Pain, inflammation, fever
Dosage: 200mg-400mg every 6-8 hours, maximum 1200mg per day
Take after food to avoid stomach irritation
Side effects: Stomach upset, ulcers with long-term use
Avoid: Taking on empty stomach; avoid if you have ulcers or kidney problems

ATORVASTATIN
Used for: High cholesterol, prevention of heart disease
Dosage: 10mg-20mg once daily, usually at night
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Muscle pain, liver enzyme changes
Avoid: Grapefruit juice, alcohol in excess

LOSARTAN
Used for: High blood pressure, protecting kidneys in diabetes
Dosage: 50mg-100mg once daily
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Dizziness, high potassium levels
Avoid: Potassium supplements without doctor advice

PANTOPRAZOLE
Used for: Acid reflux, stomach ulcers, gastritis
Dosage: 40mg once daily before breakfast
Take on empty stomach
Side effects: Headache, diarrhea
Avoid: Long-term use without monitoring

DICLOFENAC
Used for: Pain and inflammation - arthritis, muscle pain, back pain
Dosage: 50mg two to three times daily after food
Take after food to reduce stomach irritation
Side effects: Stomach upset, increased blood pressure
Avoid: If you have heart disease or stomach ulcers

RANITIDINE
Used for: Acid reflux, heartburn, stomach ulcers
Dosage: 150mg twice daily or 300mg at night
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Headache, constipation
Avoid: Long term use, consult doctor for chronic symptoms

DOMPERIDONE
Used for: Nausea, vomiting, bloating
Dosage: 10mg three times daily before meals
Take 15-30 minutes before food
Side effects: Dry mouth, headache
Avoid: Use with caution in heart conditions

ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts)
Used for: Dehydration from diarrhea or vomiting
Dosage: One sachet dissolved in 1 liter clean water, sip throughout the day
Take after each loose stool
Side effects: None at normal use
Avoid: Using contaminated water to prepare

ZINC SULFATE
Used for: Diarrhea in children, immune support
Dosage: 20mg once daily for 10-14 days (children)
Take with food to avoid stomach upset
Side effects: Mild stomach upset
Avoid: Taking with calcium-rich foods at same time

FOLIC ACID
Used for: Pregnancy supplementation, anemia prevention
Dosage: 400mcg-5mg once daily
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Rare
Avoid: None significant

IRON SUPPLEMENTS (Ferrous Sulfate)
Used for: Iron deficiency anemia, pregnancy
Dosage: 1 tablet daily as prescribed
Take with vitamin C rich food for better absorption, avoid tea/coffee with it
Side effects: Constipation, dark stools, stomach upset
Avoid: Taking with milk, tea, or coffee which reduce absorption

CALCIUM SUPPLEMENTS
Used for: Bone health, pregnancy, osteoporosis prevention
Dosage: 500mg-1000mg daily as prescribed
Take with food for better absorption
Side effects: Constipation, bloating
Avoid: Taking with iron supplements at the same time

VITAMIN D3
Used for: Bone health, vitamin D deficiency
Dosage: 60000 IU weekly or as prescribed
Take with a meal containing fat for better absorption
Side effects: Rare at prescribed doses
Avoid: Self-medicating high doses without testing

MULTIVITAMIN TABLETS
Used for: General nutrition support, weakness
Dosage: One tablet daily after food
Take after breakfast
Side effects: Rare
Avoid: Taking more than prescribed dose

COTRIMOXAZOLE
Used for: Bacterial infections - urinary tract, respiratory
Dosage: As prescribed, usually twice daily
Take with plenty of water
Side effects: Skin rash, stomach upset
Avoid: Sun exposure, ensure adequate hydration

ALBENDAZOLE
Used for: Intestinal worm infections
Dosage: 400mg single dose, repeat after 2 weeks if advised
Take after food
Side effects: Mild stomach upset
Avoid: Pregnancy without doctor consultation

MEBENDAZOLE
Used for: Intestinal worm infections in children and adults
Dosage: 100mg twice daily for 3 days, or single dose as advised
Take with food
Side effects: Mild abdominal pain
Avoid: Pregnancy without doctor advice

SALBUTAMOL (Inhaler)
Used for: Asthma, breathing difficulty, wheezing
Dosage: 1-2 puffs as needed, maximum as prescribed
Use during breathing difficulty
Side effects: Tremors, fast heartbeat
Avoid: Overuse without doctor guidance

PREDNISOLONE
Used for: Severe allergic reactions, asthma flare-ups, inflammation
Dosage: As prescribed by doctor, tapering dose
Take after food, in the morning
Side effects: Increased appetite, mood changes with long use
Avoid: Stopping suddenly without doctor advice

INSULIN (various types)
Used for: Diabetes management when oral medicines are not enough
Dosage: As prescribed by doctor, varies by type
Injected as directed, timing relative to meals varies
Side effects: Low blood sugar if overdosed
Avoid: Skipping meals after taking insulin

ASPIRIN (low dose)
Used for: Heart attack and stroke prevention
Dosage: 75mg-150mg once daily
Take after food
Side effects: Stomach irritation, bleeding risk
Avoid: If you have active stomach ulcers or bleeding disorders

CLOPIDOGREL
Used for: Prevention of blood clots after heart attack or stroke
Dosage: 75mg once daily
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Bleeding risk, bruising
Avoid: Stopping suddenly without doctor advice

LEVOTHYROXINE
Used for: Underactive thyroid (hypothyroidism)
Dosage: As prescribed, usually once daily
Take on empty stomach, 30-60 minutes before breakfast
Side effects: Rare at correct dose
Avoid: Taking with calcium or iron supplements at same time

ORAL CONTRACEPTIVE PILLS
Used for: Family planning, regulating periods
Dosage: One tablet daily at the same time
Take at the same time every day
Side effects: Nausea, mood changes, spotting
Avoid: Missing doses; consult doctor if side effects persist

MISOPROSTOL
Used for: Labor induction, postpartum hemorrhage prevention (under medical supervision)
Dosage: As prescribed by healthcare provider only
Only to be used under medical supervision
Side effects: Cramping, bleeding
Avoid: Self-medication; this drug requires medical supervision

ORS WITH ZINC (combination therapy)
Used for: Childhood diarrhea management
Dosage: ORS as needed, zinc 10-20mg daily for 14 days
Continue feeding the child normally
Side effects: Minimal
Avoid: Stopping zinc course early

VITAMIN B COMPLEX
Used for: Nerve health, weakness, fatigue
Dosage: One tablet daily after food
Take after breakfast
Side effects: Rare
Avoid: None significant

ANTACID (Aluminium Hydroxide + Magnesium Hydroxide)
Used for: Acidity, heartburn, indigestion
Dosage: 10-15ml or 1-2 tablets after meals and at bedtime
Take after food
Side effects: Constipation or diarrhea
Avoid: Taking with other medicines within 2 hours

LORATADINE
Used for: Allergies, hay fever, skin allergies
Dosage: 10mg once daily
Can be taken with or without food
Side effects: Mild drowsiness, dry mouth
Avoid: Alcohol

HYDROCHLOROTHIAZIDE
Used for: High blood pressure, fluid retention
Dosage: 12.5mg-25mg once daily in the morning
Take in the morning to avoid nighttime urination
Side effects: Increased urination, low potassium
Avoid: Taking at night
